About Edvanto

Edvanto was founded with a simple yet powerful vision: to bridge the gap between talent and opportunity in both professional and educational sectors.

Our Mission

To create meaningful connections between job seekers and employers, students and tutors, by providing a reliable, efficient, and user-friendly platform that serves the needs of our diverse community.

Our Vision

To become the leading platform in India for job placement and educational services, known for our commitment to quality, integrity, and innovation in matching the right people with the right opportunities.

Our Story

Founded by a team of experienced professionals from HR and education sectors, Edvanto emerged from the understanding that finding the right job or the right tutor shouldn't be a struggle. We leverage technology and human expertise to create perfect matches.
